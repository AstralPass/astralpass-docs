This page is a light overview for DAC leads and admins, not a full admin manual.

## What DAC admins use AstralPass for

At a high level, AstralPass helps a DAC:

- verify that members control the wallets they claim
- connect Discord accounts to wallet identity
- guide members through onboarding steps
- keep Discord roles aligned with DAC-specific rules

## The main admin idea

AstralPass is not just a role bot.
It is a rule-driven bridge between:

- Discord-side approval and roles
- wallet ownership
- Star Atlas DAC membership and related state

## Why that matters

Without a tool like AstralPass, these systems can drift apart:

- a user may be in Discord but not properly linked on-chain
- a user may have joined on-chain but not finished Discord onboarding
- a user may have linked the wrong wallet

AstralPass helps admins reduce that mismatch.

## What this page does not cover yet

This page does not go deep into rule authoring or admin-dashboard specifics.

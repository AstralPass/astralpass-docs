For many Star Atlas communities, joining a DAC is not just a Discord step.
It also has an **on-chain** side.

## The important distinction

From a user point of view, it is easy to think:

- “I joined the Discord, so I joined the DAC.”

But those are often different things.

A DAC may require:

1. a Discord-side approval step
2. an on-chain DAC join or application step
3. the correct wallet to be linked in AstralPass

## Why AstralPass cares

AstralPass needs those systems to line up.
If one of those pieces is missing, it may continue showing guidance instead of giving you the outcome you expected.

## Common reasons this step feels broken

If you already think you joined, but AstralPass still does not recognize it, common reasons include:

- the wallet you linked is not the wallet that joined the DAC
- your DAC approval is still pending
- your Discord-side step is not complete yet
- AstralPass has not re-evaluated your current state yet

## What to check first

If this is the step you are stuck on, check:

- which wallet actually joined the DAC
- whether Discord-side approval is complete
- whether AstralPass is looking at the same wallet you used on-chain

---
title: Joining a DAC On-Chain
description: Why on-chain DAC membership matters to AstralPass and how it fits into onboarding.
---

For many Star Atlas communities, joining a DAC is not only a Discord step.
It also has an **on-chain** side.

## Why AstralPass cares

AstralPass is designed to help bridge:

- Discord membership
- wallet ownership
- Star Atlas DAC membership on-chain

That means a DAC may require all of them before you fully pass its onboarding flow.

## The common pattern

A user may need to:

1. complete the Discord-side approval step
2. join or apply to the DAC on-chain
3. link the relevant wallet to AstralPass

If one of those is missing, AstralPass may keep showing guidance instead of fully granting the expected outcome.

## Why this can feel confusing

From a user point of view, it can feel like “I already joined.”
But in practice, Discord approval and on-chain DAC membership are often separate checks.

AstralPass exists partly to make those differences easier to understand.

## If you already joined and still do not pass

Possible reasons include:

- the wallet you linked is not the wallet that joined the DAC
- DAC approval is still pending
- Discord-side approval is not finished yet
- AstralPass has not re-evaluated your current state yet

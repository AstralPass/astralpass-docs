---
title: Glossary
description: Plain-language definitions for common AstralPass and Star Atlas terms used in these docs.
---

## AstralPass

A tool that helps connect Discord identity, wallet ownership, and Star Atlas-related membership or profile state.

## DAC

Short for **Decentralized Autonomous Corporation**.
In practice, this is Star Atlas' version of a more formal player-run organization, similar to a guild, clan, or alliance.

## Discord approval

Any Discord-side step a DAC may require before a user is considered fully accepted, such as rules acceptance, an application, or manual review.

## Linked wallet

A wallet that a user has connected to AstralPass and proven ownership of.

## Player Profile

A Star Atlas-native identity object that helps connect a wallet to in-game context.

## Role sync

The process of evaluating rules and updating Discord roles based on a user's current state.

## On-chain DAC membership

The part of DAC membership that exists in Star Atlas or Solana-related systems, separate from Discord server access.

## Hot wallet

A browser or software wallet that can approve requests directly in the app without a separate hardware device.

## Hardware wallet

A wallet setup that requires a separate physical device, such as a Ledger, to approve sensitive actions.

Most AstralPass users will go through a simple flow:

1. **Sign in with Discord**
2. **Connect a wallet**
3. **Approve the ownership check**
4. **Let AstralPass evaluate your current state**

From there, AstralPass may either complete your verification or guide you to the next missing step.

## What AstralPass is checking

Depending on the DAC and the current flow, AstralPass may care about:

- whether you control the Discord account
- whether you control the linked wallet
- whether the right wallet is linked
- whether a DAC-specific step is still missing
- whether your Discord and Star Atlas state are aligned

## What happens after you sign in

After you sign in and link a wallet, AstralPass can:

- update your Discord roles
- show you what is still missing
- bring you back into the right account state later

## Important: Discord approval and DAC membership are not the same thing

A common point of confusion is assuming that one completed step means everything is done.

In reality, a DAC may require several separate things, such as:
- Discord-side approval
- on-chain DAC membership
- wallet linking

AstralPass helps make those differences visible.

If you are ready to connect a wallet, continue with [Linking Wallets](/core/linking-wallets/).

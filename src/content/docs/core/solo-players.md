---
title: Solo Players
description: How AstralPass can still be useful if you are not part of a DAC.
---

AstralPass was built for DACs first, but it is not limited to DAC members.

If you are a solo player, AstralPass can still be useful as a place to connect:

- your Discord identity
- one or more wallets
- Star Atlas profile-related context

## What solo players can still do

A solo player can still:

1. sign in with Discord
2. link one or more wallets
3. return to the same account state later
4. use player-profile and dashboard-related features where available

## What may not apply

If you are not part of a DAC, some DAC-specific onboarding or role-sync behavior may simply not apply to you.
That does not mean something is broken.

It usually just means the DAC-oriented parts of AstralPass are not relevant for your current use case.

## Practical takeaway

If you are a solo player, focus first on:

- signing in with Discord
- linking the right wallet or wallets
- understanding your Star Atlas profile context

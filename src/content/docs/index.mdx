## AstralPass in one sentence

AstralPass helps DACs and players connect **Discord identity**, **wallet ownership**, and **Star Atlas membership or profile data** in one place.

## Before you troubleshoot

A lot of AstralPass questions come down to one of these:

- the wrong wallet was linked
- a DAC step is still incomplete
- Discord approval and on-chain DAC membership are not the same thing
- a role has not been re-evaluated yet

If that sounds familiar, jump to [Troubleshooting](/core/troubleshooting/).
